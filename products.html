Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and ordering process

How do I order via WhatsApp?
Simply click any "Order Now" button on our website. This will open WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing product details. Just send the message and our team will respond within minutes to confirm your order and provide pricing.
What are your delivery areas?
We deliver across Canada including Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Calgary, Ottawa, Edmonton, Winnipeg, and all major cities. Shipping is available to both residential and commercial addresses.
How do I store your frozen products?
Store at -18°C or below in your freezer. Keep products frozen until ready to use. Do not refreeze after thawing. For best results, cook from frozen state.
Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes! We offer volume discounts for restaurants, caterers, and wholesale buyers. Contact us via WhatsApp for bulk pricing and custom packaging options.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept e-transfer, credit cards, and cash on delivery (available in select areas). Payment details are shared after order confirmation via WhatsApp.

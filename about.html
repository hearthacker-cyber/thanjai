Our Story

Bringing Authentic Indian Flavors to Canada Since 2010

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in frozen foods, our journey is one of passion, quality, and dedication to authentic taste.

Our Journey

From a small family kitchen to serving thousands of Canadian families

2010

The Beginning

Founded in Montreal with a single product line - traditional samosas. Started supplying to local Indian grocery stores in Quebec area.

2013

First Major Expansion

Opened our first CFIA-approved manufacturing facility. Expanded product line to include parottas and began supplying to major supermarket chains.

2016

Going National

Established distribution network across Canada. Partnered with logistics companies for temperature-controlled nationwide delivery.

2019

Quality Recognition

Awarded "Best Frozen Food Manufacturer" by Canadian Food Industry Association. Achieved HACCP and ISO 22000 certifications.

2023

Today & Beyond

Serving over 500 retail partners across Canada. Continuously innovating with new product lines while maintaining traditional recipes.

Our Mission

To bring authentic Indian culinary experiences to Canadian households through premium quality frozen foods, while maintaining traditional recipes and uncompromising quality standards.

  • Preserve traditional Indian cooking methods
  • Use 100% natural ingredients
  • Ensure food safety at every step
  • Make authentic Indian food accessible

Our Vision

To become Canada's most trusted brand for authentic ethnic frozen foods, expanding our reach while staying true to our roots and quality commitments.

  • Expand product range to include more regional specialties
  • Establish presence in every Canadian province
  • Introduce sustainable packaging by 2025
  • Maintain leadership in food safety and quality
